Shopify vs Magento vs WooCommerce ‚Äì Which is the Best Platform For eCommerce

The basic dream and goal of any business entrepreneur are to sell all the goods in a safe and secure platform that is less complicated and serve the customers as this is the only way to grow the business. In the digital world in order to withstand the competition one needs to come up with a wonderful online store to sell products and services through a safe platform. Basing on the business needs, budget and other attributes, picking the eCommerce platform comfort the users as boosting sales and losing customers completely depends on the decision of the entrepreneurs.

Launching a business requires a safe and worthy platform that is much functional as it is necessary to have customized, easy to install or maintain services. Several platforms create great confusion for the eCommerce business entrepreneurs as every platform is designed with uniqueness and comfort. Having a tough time choosing between Shopify, Magento, and WooCommerce here we try to explain to you the features and uniqueness of the popular eCommerce platforms.

The basic features that make any platform popular and user-friendly is the procedure to build a store and the easy to manage features which are essential in working of the site. On average everyone looks for the powerful platform that is not much complicated to use and the one which brings life to your vision. Looking for the one that is flexible as well as simple to use requires quality research and with affordable monthly fee solves the greatest worry. All the platforms come with pros and cons, but choosing one that satisfies the requirements wisely allows the business of every volume to attain success.

Shopify

Shopify is one of the old and worthy places to build a store as they are experienced in serving people since 2004 with innovative methods. This platform is supportive of stores of various volumes and boosts the profits of every business. The easy to use and thorough assistance makes Shopify special and never allows its user stuck due to which this is one special platform to serve digital entrepreneurs.

Features

Shopify is the easiest platform to deal with as it supports users in various ways and it comes with multiple benefits including professional-looking themes.

  • The customer support attends immediately and solves the problem and never fails in delivering satisfactory solutions.
  • The best part of Shopify is that they take complete care of maintaining, and updating the software which is not seen on the rest of the platforms.
  • Setting up a store through this high-quality hosting service allows you to serve through a safe and secure cart.

With the monthly fee users can start their store and the platform offers nearly ten themes for free which are not much complex offering the required look. The best part is that the Shopify themes are extremely catchy and are easily customized if necessary. By choosing the apps that cause benefit to business one can enjoy the success of their store as it supports in growing. Managing your business is easy with Shopify as it is one completely cloud-based server. It is easy to use your own domain name with Shopify and with an exclusive Shopify plan selling of all your products is simple.

Ideal for the business of all volumes

  • Sign up for Shopify and start your business set up to sell all your products and payments are transparent and depend on the third-party payment processor.
  • Earning a hold on the market is actually tough for a beginner and with the powerful platform, Shopify one can focus much on serving the customers as it is secured and much flexible.
  • It is completely designed with advanced functionalities and due to this management, the orders are not tough and the back end supports thoroughly in maintenance.
  • With three different plans, Shopify serves beginners, people in growing as well as an advanced stage of business as per the need.

The basic advantage of Shopify is that the apps are easy to install and beginners find the setup simple and manageable.

Right support

In the present trend selling is seen almost everywhere, which includes social media sites, marketplaces and many more and Shopify supports in marketing. Run your store in a stress-free manner as Shopify allows you to focus much on extending your business wisely. Shopify offers various apps with wide uses, including blogging, live chat, secure shopping, tracking orders and many others that are exclusively designed for the growth of the store.

Start with the 14-day free trial and learn more about Shopify, which helps a lot in understanding the working. Though Shopify plans are a bit expensive they offer unlimited advantages and are perfect for people looking for many functional solutions.

Magento

Working with Magento is one safe method to build a successful online store and boosting sales is even possible with the secured platform. The store grows widely within no time with this flexible platform which gives much freedom in designing your online store. This is one of the most customized eCommerce platforms and is open-source which provides a single click installation. Building an online business site with Magento is worthy as it offers management tools along with SEO.

Solve all your problems of designing a special and attractive store as Magneto is loaded with featured solutions. With a single admin handling, multiple stores is easy as Magento supports in having various pages and the best part is that every site owes a catchy look. In order to enjoy hosting freedom with unique functioning, it is necessary to have a proper working of resources from your end.

Core functional

Building a much functional store is possible and Magento offers a worthy chance to utilize unlimited types of shades, font and style.

  • Rewriting URLs for SEO services and meta descriptions is possible with Magento and this is one of the finest eCommerce platforms that.
  • With the impressive list of features, one creating a much functional store is easy, but handling this is a bit tough for a non-technical person.
  • It offers a fair chance in creating a website with quality themes, extensions and site design which ensures smooth functioning.
  • A technically skilled person can work with Magento easily and can create their own hosting as this is the most flexible.

There are two editions through which mostly Magento serves the customers in which one is completely paid and the other comes for free to install. The enterprise edition is a bit pricey and is not for people who are beginners and entrepreneurs need to be extremely careful as it requires people with technical skills. Enhancing the functionalities is possible only with Magento as getting dedicated landing pages for all products is offered here.

Magento comes with built-in functionality and is scalable as this supports in multistore or website handling without any break. It is preferred as this is much SEO friendly and owes core functionality due to which it improves store organic ranking. In order to maintain smoothness in work, it is suggested to have a developer with quality technical knowledge to manage the site efficiently on this platform. People planning to make the store with less development costs need to be technically strong and running the store successfully without assistance is actually tough. As Magento is one flexible platform this is the perfect choice for an eCommerce store due to its exceptional abilities.

WooCommerce

Customize your online store with WooCommerce as this stands as an aid in website development and maintenance. Right from 2011 WooCommerce is proudly serving people who love to have an online store and is even ensuring success playing a major role in the growth of the business. Any store or website created with this platform as added to a WordPress site and due to this, it is great for business. As this offers full control on the content grabbing attention through efficient content helps in gaining organic traffic.

For people who love freedom

WooCommerce is considered to be the most easily customized eCommerce platform to build an online store, especially for people looking for low cost solutions.

  • People looking to create pages, blogs, and news of the brand can fulfill their dreams with WooCommerce as this is the best choice.
  • Launching the eCommerce store through WooCommerce is simple as they offer several extensions and payment gateways.
  • Save time as well as money with WooCommerce as they allow shipping costs according to the delivery with reliable as well as secured sources.
  • Simple and powerful solutions are easily available at WooCommerce and people looking to enjoy success through the online store can use it and appear in the initial pages of the search engine.
  • The best part is that WooCommerce is compatible with a majority of the versions of word press and is easy to install.

WooCommerce is much convenient to use as it helps in listing as well as management and has the highest SEO score. It is less expensive than compared with Shopify and it does not offer thorough support throughout the day. In WooCommerce the complete control of the codes is based on customizations and this has no transaction fees as if other platforms. This platform requires excess maintenance and doesn‚Äôt support through emails as the rest of the platforms. WooCommerce is not very safe due to the lack of maintenance and this lacks core functionality.

Selling anything is possible with WooCommerce as this is much more flexible and supports various stores to start and win a place. WooCommerce monitors the changes and helps in updating as well as bug fixing, but one needs to have some knowledge of coding to handle complicated situations.

Which is better – Shopify vs Magento vs WooCommerce?

Uniqueness is seen in every platform along with the business growth, but it is a must to understand the features before finalizing to enjoy the benefits thoroughly. With timely updates, regular maintenance and proper support one can easily manage the store. But every platform has a few pros as well as cons so deciding which is a better option depends on the requirement of the store.

  • Due to the complex and high functionality, Magento is not very much user-friendly and the ease for beginners is very less when compared to that of Shopify and WooCommerce.
  • Though Magento is complex to use when compared to Shopify and WooCommerce with assistance handling it is not complicated.
  • Shopify improves site speed and performance when compared to that of WooCommerce and Magneto s stores looking for speed can pick it.
  • When it comes to customer support Shopify gains better points that Magento and WooCommerce as this serve customers in many ways.
  • Customization is necessary for every business and in this case, Magento offers many options than WooCommerce and Shopify.
  • Boosting sales require ease to work with the platform and here Shopify wins as this is designed to support people without technical knowledge.
  • WooCommerce is less expensive than Shopify and Magento and it provides nearly 100 themes to make the site impressive.
  • In order to earn organic traffic, effective SEO is essential and Shopify lacks in SEO when compared to that of Magento and WooCommerce.

Shopify is apt for beginners and one with small businesses as it supports the smooth running of the business. Whereas Magento helps in growing with medium or big stores who like to conquer the market as it supports with impressive SEO performance. For entrepreneurs with better coding or technical knowledge, WooCommerce is a perfect choice as one can create customized sites with fewer expenses.

Every platform is unique and comes with various interesting and customized features that help in handling the website easily. All three eCommerce platforms have their own pros and cons starting from ease, cost, support, performance, safety and many others.

Boosting the sales and managing the store in a personalized manner is possible only with the right choice, so stay cautious and pick one that satisfies your needs within your budget as the major goal is to earn profits.
