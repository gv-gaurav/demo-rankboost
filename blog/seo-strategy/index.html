1. Link-Building Strategies

(i). Use Facebook group polls to create linkable research posts:

Facebook group polls can be a great way to quickly build backlinks. You can use them to create linkable research posts and generate interest in your content. Focus on creating polls that are interesting and relevant to your target audience. This can be an effective way to build backlinks from authoritative domains.

Find groups with 1000+ members and create polls with questions that are related to your content. This will give you the best chance of getting high-quality, authoritative backlinks for your website. gather original insights from the data you collect and use them to create compelling content that can be shared across multiple platforms.

(ii). Create articles on the most-searched industry statistics:

Research the most-searched topics and industry statistics in your niche, then create an article that has a unique take on them. These types of articles can be highly effective for SEO as they are often shared by other websites and publishers.

Make sure to include keywords relevant to your target audience in the article title, headings, and content. This will help improve the visibility of your article on search engine results pages (SERPs). The majority of people prefer reading articles that are written in an engaging and easy-to-understand manner. So make sure to avoid using overly technical language and break down complex concepts.

(iii). Gain top positions by ranking on low-competition phrases:

In order to rank in top positions of SERPs, you need to focus your SEO efforts on low-competition phrases. Try to identify keywords that are not overly competitive but still have high search volumes. These types of terms are often referred to as “long tail keywords”.

Optimizing for these long-tail keywords will help improve your rankings and boost your website’s visibility on SERPs. You can use the best SEO tools such as Google Trends and Keyword Planner to find the best keywords for your niche.

In addition, make sure to include these terms in the titles of your articles and add them naturally into your content whenever possible. This will help increase the chances of your website ranking for these terms, resulting in higher visibility and more traffic.

(iv). Do podcast interviews on high DA websites:

Podcasts are one of the most popular content formats right now and they can be very effective for SEO. Reach out to high-DA websites in your niche and request to be a guest on their podcast. This will help you build relationships with industry influencers, as well as get backlinks from authoritative domains.

Make sure to prepare for the interview by researching the topics that are likely to be discussed. This will give you the best chance of creating an engaging and informative podcast episode that can be shared across multiple platforms.

(v). Promote your top linkable content with targeted ads:

In addition to using organic tactics, you can also leverage paid methods to promote your content. Targeted ads are one of the most effective ways to spread awareness about your linkable content and get more backlinks.

You can use Facebook Ads Manager or Google Ads to create campaigns for your top-performing pieces of content. Make sure to optimize your ads for the most relevant keywords in your niche and target them to people who are likely to be interested in your content. This will help you get more visits and generate more backlinks.

2. Keyword Research Tactics

(i). Use estimated traffic potential than single-phrase search volume:

Rather than just focusing on the single phrase search volume for individual keywords, you should also consider the estimated traffic potential of keyword combinations. This is a great way to identify high-value keywords that are likely to drive more organic traffic.

To make it easier, single-phrase keyword volume means the total search volume for any given keyword. Estimated traffic potential, on the other hand, is the estimated amount of organic traffic that can be generated by leveraging a combination of related terms. The more you focus on accurate keyword research, the better your chances of creating effective SEO strategies that deliver results in 2024.

(ii). Search for top-ranking URLs with zero backlinks:

Another great way to identify high-value keywords is to look for top-ranking URLs with zero backlinks. This means that the page ranks on SERPs despite not having any external backlinks pointing toward it. You can use SEO tools such as Ahrefs or SEMrush to find these pages and uncover their associated keyword phrases.

Once you analyze the pages and understand the phrases they’re ranking for, you can use these terms to optimize your content. This is a great way to improve your rankings and get more organic traffic from SERPs. This process might take time to find such URLs but once you do, you’ll be able to create effective content using them that will rank quickly.

(iii). Don’t rank on SERPs that have a local pack (they have the lowest organic CTR):

When creating your SEO strategy, it’s important to consider the SERP features that are likely to have an impact on your rankings. One of the most common SERP features today is the local pack, which displays a list of local businesses related to a specific search query.

These listings have very low organic CTR so it’s best to avoid ranking for these types of SERPs. Instead, focus on optimizing your content for keywords that are likely to display a carousel or featured snippet instead. This will help you get more organic traffic and help you create effective SEO strategies that deliver results in 2024.

(iv). Identify keyword clusters and build content around them:

Keyword clusters are related phrases that can be used to create more comprehensive topics. When you identify these clusters, you can use them to build content that is optimized for multiple related phrases. This will improve your chances of ranking on SERPs and increase the amount of organic traffic you’re able to generate.

To identify keyword clusters, use tools such as Ahrefs or Moz to find related terms for any given keyword phrase. This will help you create comprehensive topics that are optimized for multiple related phrases. When building content around these clusters, make sure to include all relevant terms in the content so that it can rank for multiple phrases. This is one of the best ways to create effective SEO strategies that deliver results in 2024.

3. Content Creation Strategies

(i). Update the content from the tail-end as it keeps the original structure:

One of the best ways to create effective SEO strategies is to maintain and update your existing content. This will help you maintain your rankings on SERPs and also help you to rank for more keywords. When updating existing content, it’s important to keep the original structure intact and focus on adding more value to the content. This will help you improve your rankings without having to start from scratch.

Try to update the content from the tail-end as this will make it easier to keep the original structure intact. Additionally, you can also add more content to make sure that your content is more comprehensive and covers all relevant topics.

(ii). Write shorter paragraphs to rank for featured snippets:

Another great way to create effective SEO strategies is to focus on optimizing content for featured snippets. These are short answers that appear at the top of SERPs and can greatly improve your organic CTR.

To rank for featured snippets, you should aim to write shorter paragraphs and include a few relevant images or videos in the content. This will help you stand out from the competition and improve your chances of ranking for featured snippets.

Additionally, you should also use a conversational tone when writing content as this helps to engage users and can help improve rankings. Keep the paragraph length between 40 to 50 words so that it is easier for search engines to scan the content and rank it for featured snippets.

(iii). Add FAQs at the end:

You should also include FAQs at the end of your content as this can improve user experience and help you rank for more keywords. Try to include questions that are relevant to the topic being discussed in the content and make sure to provide detailed answers. This will help search engines understand what your content is about and it will also help users find the answers they are looking for.

Additionally, you can also provide links to other relevant content or resources that can help users find the information they need. FAQs should be added at the end of the content and should include relevant keywords to help optimize your content for search engines.

(iv). Create stats-based infographics with a 16:9 aspect ratio:

Infographics are a great way to create engaging content that can be easily shared on social media and help you increase your organic reach. To make sure your infographics stand out, use an aspect ratio of 16:9 as this is the standard size used in most digital platforms.

Additionally, you should also try to include relevant stats or figures in the infographics to make them more compelling. This will help you get the attention of users and also improve your chances of ranking for relevant keywords. Make sure to add a relevant title, description, and keywords to the infographic so that search engines can easily crawl it.

4. Internal linking Strategies

(i). Link low-ranking resources with the highest-ranking pages:

When creating an internal linking strategy, make sure to link low-ranking resources with the highest-ranking pages. This will help you pass on some of your link juice and potentially improve the rankings of those pages. Additionally, it can also help users find related content that they might be interested in.

You should also link similar topics together so that your content is better organized and easier to navigate. This will help you improve the user experience and also make sure that your content is properly optimized for search engines.

(ii). Optimize anchor text and use keywords:

When creating internal links, make sure to use relevant keywords in the anchor text. This will help search engines understand the context of your content and also help you rank for more keywords. Additionally, it can also help users immediately understand what the linked page is about.

You should also use descriptive words in the anchor text as this makes it easier for search engines to crawl and index your content. Try to limit the number of links per page so that the content is not distracting and users can easily find the information they are looking for.

(iii). Link all new pages with at least 2 old pages:

For every new page that you create, make sure to link it with at least 2 old pages. This helps search engines understand the context and can help you improve rankings. Additionally, linking related topics together can also help users find all the relevant information they need in one place.

Make sure to use descriptive anchor texts for internal linking as this can help search engines understand the content and also make it easier for users to find information quickly. Moreover, it can also help you rank for more keywords.

Wrapping up!

Creating effective SEO strategies is essential to ensure that your content gets the visibility it deserves and helps you rank for more keywords. To get started, optimize your content by using relevant keywords and descriptive titles & descriptions. Additionally, add FAQs at the end of your content and also include internal links with relevant anchor texts.

You should also aim to create engaging infographics with stats and figures to draw users’ attention and improve your chances of ranking. Lastly, make sure to link new pages with at least 2 old pages so that the content is properly organized and easy to read.

Tips To Create Result-Driven SEO Strategy in 2024

As we move towards 2024, online competition is getting stiffer and harder. Business owners need to improvise their SEO strategies to get the best out of the optimization. Whether it’s an E-Commerce website, a personal branding website, or an enterprise website. It’s important to think of how to improve the overall scalability and the impact of SEO.

It’s important to have a robust result-driven SEO strategy that will help you gain an edge over others and thrive on better results. Whether you need more business leads, and traffic, or improve the overall business branding, it’s important to keep an eye on the market trends. 

If you are new to SEO and looking for a comprehensive guide, then we have got you covered. In this article, we will have a look at some result-driven and robust tips to create the best SEO strategy in 2024. As the competition is getting harder, it has become critical to stay ahead of all the remaining relevant in the business domain. 

If you wonder if all websites need the best SEO strategy, then the answer is yes. Regardless of which domain you’re in, search engine optimization is the only cost-effective way to stay ahead of you. Offline marketing is expensive, and you can’t measure the real ROI through offline campaigns. To accelerate the marketing efforts, you must focus on building a robust SEO strategy.

Whether you are new to search optimization or doing it for a while, this comprehensive guide will help you a lot. We’ve done all the research so you don’t have to do the leg work and invest time in finding the right strategy.

1. Set clear goals

The first step towards creating robust SEO tactics and strategy is to define clear goals about what you want from your SEO strategy. Whether you want to drive more traffic, need more business leads, or focus on improving the overall branding. Once you decide, it becomes easier for you to keep track of how well the strategies are working for you. Many businesses commit the mistake of not having any goals concerning search engine optimization. It ultimately results in dissatisfaction because they are not clear about what exactly they need from the SEO services. 

Sit with your team and discuss what are the major pain points and opportunities that you want to solve and capture with SEO services. With a quick brainstorming, it will become easier for you to prioritize the goals. 

2. Focus on scalability

Many businesses never think of the scalability of their SEO implementation and they end up getting stuck at a particular point. With every passing day, the customers will grow, and to cater to their demands, your services or products will also grow. So, the overall SEO strategy should be scalable so that you can ensure the indexability is faster. 

Some business websites have thousands of pages after some time. As the crawl budget is finite, search engines can only crawl certain pages. To deal with such challenges, you need to consider these points and then prepare the best SEO strategy. Be it business leads, generating traction, or getting traffic, it’s important there is a scalability factor in the implementation. 

3. Competitive analysis

Instead of doing all the research from scratch, you can dive into comprehensive competitive analysis and check what they are doing. Take out the list of the top ten competitors of your business and then check what SEO implementations they’ve done. Check the number of keywords they’re ranking for. Along with this, also check how much content they are publishing and what’s their average word count. There are several things that you can pick that are important for your business. 

You can use SEO tools to scan your competitors’ websites and also check their backlink profiles. By checking their backlinks, you will get an idea about where you also need to create backlinks and outperform them. 

4. Keyword Research

Keyword research and analysis are two important aspects that you need to follow today and in 2024. If you are starting now and just building the website, you first need to focus on low-hanging fruits. In easy terms, there is no need to go after highly competitive short keywords as it will take a lot of time and even years to rank for. Instead, start from low competition & high volume keywords that will help you gain success.

Once you start ranking on these low-competition keywords, it will become easier to optimize your website for shorter and highly competitive keywords. Also, check your competitors’ keywords and how they are ranking in top positions. Try to reverse engineer their strategy and improvise a better way out for it. 

5. Build Content Topics

You need to be clear about what topics and clusters you are going to create that will help you rank faster. For this, you need to understand your industry inside out. You need to know the pain points of your customers and creating content for its solution will land you success. The content creation process should have a bigger purpose rather than just doing it for search optimization. 

Once you decide on the core topics, you can start creating shorter clusters that will help you rank on multiple keywords. Go to Google and type the main keywords and you will find a lot of questions regarding that and its goldmine for content topics. Start picking the relevant and most searched topics so that you can cover them in more detail to outperform competitors. 

6. Picking the Right Keywords

Keyword research is one thing but picking up the right keywords takes the entire thing beyond. As we discussed earlier, don’t target the highly competitive keywords directly. Instead, start optimizing the website content with low-competition keywords. Be it service pages, landing pages, or writing blogs, the SEO strategies for 2024 should be aligned with the right keywords. It will help you get the best benefits from the overall search optimization implementation. 

Never try to over-optimize the content with excessive keywords. Regardless of what you are building, the keyword density should be around 1% to 2%. By maintaining this, you can improve the overall user experience and overall readability. So, pick the right keywords and ensure that they’re not looking forcefully added to the content. 

7. Focus on Search Intent

With the advancing search engine algorithm, search intent has become a critical aspect. As a single keyword can have multiple meanings, you need to understand what exactly your customers are searching for. It will help you write more personalized content that will bring out the maximum traction to you. 

To understand the search intent better before using a keyword, you only need to search it on Google and observe the results. You will find queries by users that will give you an idea about how you personalize the content in a much better manner. When you focus more on search intent, there are higher chances that you rank higher than your competitors. 

8. Define Your ROIs

Today and in 2024, you must keep a track of the overall ROI of your SEO efforts. It will help you understand which strategies are working and what needs improvisations. You can set custom goals in Google Analytics based on your ROI and get a better overview of it. And, the best way to track down ROIs is to manage them separately. 

In simple terms, track your traffic, revenue, leads, and other metrics separately to have a better understanding. There are many third-party tools available in the market that can help you track things better. With search engine algorithms changing rapidly, it becomes critical that you keep track of your ROI and how well you are able to achieve it. 

9. Focus on Customer Pain Points

The whole point of doing any business boils down to one thing — solving your customers’ pain points. Be it an offline business or an online business if you are able to solve customers’ problems with excellence and with faster speed, outperforming competitors becomes easier. It will help you get ahead of the curve without spending aggressively on ads and marketing. Solve your customer problems and let the solutions reach a maximum number of people through SEO. 

Once you do it, your business will never run out of leads and traffic. However, keep an eye on the market trends and how customers are changing their buying patterns. Shaping up your SEO strategy around offering solutions to your customer’s pain points is the key to a powerful strategy in 2024. 

Final thoughts

Get started with powerful SEO implementation today and for 2024 and drive more traffic, traction, and business leads to your business. If you are not leveraging SEO to the fullest, it’s the right time to hire a professional company and start working on your brand value. In 2024, the SEO will be around improved UX and accurate information. Focus on improving these elements and your business will become unstoppable. Keep these SEO tips in 2024 in your mind while building a new strategy for your business.
