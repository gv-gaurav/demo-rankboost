Quality vs Quantity Link Building: Which is More Important?

A lot of business owners mistakenly believe that quantity trumps quality. That’s because the absence of links reflects badly on your domain and website authority.

Low Volume, High Quality

Some take that to mean that link building is all about linking as many sites to your pages as possible. Until you saw soon enough that having a lot of links won’t do any good to your bottom line, not if they’re not quality links. That’s why quality is the most essential consideration in building links to your site.

High Volume, Low Quality

However, that doesn’t mean quantity doesn’t matter. If you’re spending all your time to get one quality link over fifty “okay” links, that’s not a good use of your resources either.
