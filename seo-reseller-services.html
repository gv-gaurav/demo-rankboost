SEO Reseller Guide for Agencies & professionals

SEO reselling may seem complex, but it's not as tricky as it sounds. An SEO Reseller helps agencies outsource SEO services for their clients. This lets agencies focus on their strengths, save time and resources, build better client relationships, and offer industry-level SEO solutions under their brand name.

With EZ Rankings, it's even simpler. Curious about common reseller SEO terms? Here are some key terms to know:

  • What is an SEO Reseller?
  • How An SEO Reseller Company Help?
  • What is the Cost of SEO Reseller Agency

What is an SEO Reseller?

An SEO Reseller serves as an outsourcing partner for agencies (typically design, development, and marketing agencies) that let them offer SEO service to their clients without hiring an in-house SEO team. When agencies partner with a white label SEO Reseller, they can use the Reseller's SEO expertise to serve clients, all under the agency's own branding. This partnership allows agencies to expand their business & profit as well.

How Can An SEO Reseller Company Help?

The best SEO Resellers help agencies outsource the entire SEO process to trusted experts, freeing up time to acquire new clients and excel in their core competencies. When executed correctly, this creates a mutually beneficial for both partners. Agencies can assign their clients' SEO work to the Reseller, avoiding the need for additional resources, all while maintaining their brand identity. Additionally, SEO Resellers often offer white-label services, ensuring the work is carried out under the agency's brand name.

What is the Cost of SEO Reseller Agency

Pricing is determined based on the scope of work, and it can vary from one agency to another. Typically, SEO Resellers do not disclose their costs online, requiring agencies to reach out directly for accurate pricing.

According to Google data, SEO costs are highly variable, ranging from as low as $1,000 to well over $100,000. On average, SEO agency rates fall between $50 and $500 per hour. The varied pricing options suit different budgets, allowing both small businesses and larger companies to access SEO services that match their particular needs and goals.
