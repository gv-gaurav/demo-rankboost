Initial Review & Analysis

On-Page Optimization

On-page optimization is a key element in any digital marketing price package. It focuses on optimizing the individual web page content, HTML code, images, and other elements to make your website more search engine friendly. This involves optimizing title tags, meta descriptions, and headings to boost relevance to search engine algorithms. Additionally, creating user-friendly and search-engine-friendly URLs is part of the process. Considering the growing importance on mobile usage, digital marketing packages also include optimization for mobile devices, ensuring a comprehensive marketing approach across all platforms.

When the on-page optimization is done right, you'll begin to see a steady rise in your rankings and organic traffic. At EZ Rankings, we understand the importance of both on-page and off-page optimization and ensure that your digital marketing pricing packages include them.

Local Search Optimization (If Applicable)

Local search optimization refers to the process of optimizing your website for local search results. This includes targeting location-specific keywords, setting up a Google My Business account, and submitting listings to local directories. As the competition among businesses continues to increase, it’s essential to make sure that you’re seen in local search results. Local search optimization helps you reach more potential customers who are looking for services in your area. Activities within local SEO include optimizing profiles on platforms like GBP, Yelp, and Bing Places for Business. Additionally, it involves submitting your website to local directories such as Yellow Pages, SuperPages, and CitySearch

At EZ Rankings, our team of years of experience in local search optimization will help you get found in the right places. Furthermore, local SEO allows you to target customers in specific areas, giving you a competitive edge over other businesses.
